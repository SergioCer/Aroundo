What is Aroundo?

Aroundo connects people, places and experiences. It is a collaborative platform where everyone can contribute to creating a better map of events and opportunities.

User

Users can discover events, explore places, save experiences and participate in the Aroundo community.

Creator

Creators are people who create experiences and content: artists, professionals, cultural operators and anyone who wants to share something meaningful.

Organizer

Organizers create and manage events, coordinating places, creators and participants.

Place Manager

Place Managers are responsible for places available on Aroundo. They maintain information about locations and manage their availability.

Trust & Community

Aroundo is based on trust. The platform provides tools, but the quality of the experience comes from the people who use it.
