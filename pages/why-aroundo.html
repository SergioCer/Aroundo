What is Aroundo?

Aroundo is a network of people, places and experiences. It connects those who want to discover events with those who create them, organize them or make the places where they happen available.

Aroundo grows through the contribution of its community: every person can add value, share knowledge and help create a more reliable and complete map of experiences.

How can I participate?

Aroundo does not define people by a single category. A person can have different roles over time and participate in different ways.

You can start simply as a User and, whenever you want, add new roles according to what you do and what you want to contribute.

User

A User is someone who wants to explore the territory and discover new experiences.

Users can:

  • discover events and places
  • save interesting experiences
  • participate in activities
  • share feedback with the community
Creator

Creators are the people who create experiences and content to share with the community.

Creators may include artists, professionals, cultural operators, performers or anyone who offers something meaningful to others.

A Creator brings the experience to life.

Organizer

Organizers are responsible for creating and coordinating events.

They connect places, creators and participants, providing the information needed to make an experience available on Aroundo.

Place Manager

A Place Manager is responsible for a place available on Aroundo.

The role is not necessarily linked to ownership: it represents the person or organization responsible for maintaining the information and availability of that place.

A place can be created by the community and, over time, its responsibility can be assigned to the appropriate manager.

Service Provider

Service Providers offer skills and support that help make events possible.

Examples include technical services, equipment, production support and other professional contributions.

Trust & Community

Trust is the foundation of Aroundo.

The platform provides the tools, but the quality of the experience depends on the people who use them.

Accurate information, respect for places and events, and collaboration between users are what make the network grow.
